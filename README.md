## About Me

I'm **Robert Kimutai**, a graduate engineer and data scientist based in Kenya. I design and build **end-to-end systems** at the intersection of engineering, data science, and sustainable development — from a national health insurance platform architected for **54 million citizens** to IoT sensor networks that replace **\$10,000 lab equipment for under \$100**.

-   :mortar_board: **MSc** Environmental & Biosystems Engineering — University of Nairobi
-   :kenya: Registered Graduate Engineer — **EBK #B16657**
-   :seedling: Currently building AI-augmented development workflows as standard practice
